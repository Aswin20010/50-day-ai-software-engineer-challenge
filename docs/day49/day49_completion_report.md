# 21. Day 50 Risk List

## Risk 1 – Marking Research as Completed Too Early

Mitigation:

* State that research preparation is complete.
* State that empirical evaluation and publication remain pending.

## Risk 2 – Overstating Project Validation

Mitigation:

* Record actual command results.
* Include warnings and skipped tests.
* Do not label skipped browser validation as passed.

## Risk 3 – Publishing Confidential Information

Mitigation:

* Use synthetic examples.
* Review screenshots.
* Search repositories for secrets and internal identifiers.

## Risk 4 – Incomplete README Instructions

Mitigation:

* Test setup commands in a clean environment where practical.
* Remove obsolete commands.
* Document required versions.

## Risk 5 – Research Scope Too Broad

Mitigation:

* Use a minimum viable evaluation first.
* Limit the first paper to the implemented architecture and selected cases.

## Risk 6 – Weak Evidence for Novelty

Mitigation:

* Complete the literature review before finalizing novelty claims.
* Frame contribution around integration and governance.

## Risk 7 – Unresolved Playwright Errors

Mitigation:

* Document browser validation as skipped or partial.
* Keep unit, lint, build, and manual-browser evidence separate.
* Revisit Playwright after the final roadmap if required.
