# Research Problem Definition

## Problem Statement

Enterprise API development requires engineers to manually transform business requirements into implementation artifacts such as workflows and code-generation specifications.

This process is time-consuming, requires specialized knowledge, and introduces inconsistencies across implementations.

---

## Research Question

Can AI automate the transformation of enterprise requirements into implementation-ready artifacts while maintaining accuracy and reducing engineering effort?

---

## Scope

### Project 1

**Confluence → Node-RED Flow**

Automatically generate executable Node-RED workflows from enterprise business requirements documented in Confluence.

### Project 2

**Confluence + Node-RED Flow → Excel + Gap Analysis**

Automatically generate factory-ingestable Excel specifications and identify missing or inconsistent information by analyzing both requirements and generated workflows.

---

## Expected Benefits

* Faster development cycles
* Reduced manual effort
* Improved implementation consistency
* Better documentation quality
* Accelerated software modernization initiatives
